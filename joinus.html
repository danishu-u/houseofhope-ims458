Volunteer/Partner with Us
At the House of Hope, we value contributions in kind and in the form of time, love and commitment, be it at the individual or corporate level.
We have been blessed to benefit from and to see work-from-home mothers share their love and time with our children; retired school teachers come in and tutor our children;
retirees come in and help in our kitchen; and corporations come in and conduct activities ranging from skills development to day camps.
