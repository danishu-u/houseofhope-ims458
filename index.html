House of Hope

Where the poor get help, the hurting are comforted, and the desperate find hope.

We can make a difference.

The House of Hope is a charity that journeys with families in Penang affected by urban poverty. We provide food aid, community support, distribute financial protection, and run educational programs. We work to see children, mothers, fathers, and the elderly live with dignity, love and hope for their future.

Poverty affects everything.

There are children, mothers, fathers, and the elderly around us who struggle to have enough to eat. There are elderly who struggle with loneliness and a broken heart from losing loved ones. There are fathers and mothers struggling to provide, family members struggling to care for children, and children lost without the guidance and love they need.
